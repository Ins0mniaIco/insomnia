Insomnia

The Discord bot that is smarter then your avarage bot, just bind the AI or DM it to talk with it.

    Admin Commands

  • insomnia blacklist
    Blacklists or un-blacklists users and guilds from the bot.
  • insomnia conf
    Define per-guild configuration.
  • insomnia eval
    Evaluates arbitrary Javascript. Reserved for bot owner.
  • insomnia load
    Load a piece from your bot.
  • insomnia reboot
    Reboots the bot.
  • insomnia reload
    Reloads a klasa piece, or all pieces of a klasa store.
  • insomnia transfer
    Transfers a core piece to its respective folder.
  • insomnia unload
    Unloads the klasa piece.

    General Commands

  • insomnia help
    Display help for a command.
  • insomnia invite
    Displays the join server link of the bot.
  • insomnia ping
    Runs a connection test to Discord.

    • User Config

  • insomnia userconf
    Define per-user configuration.

    Configuration Commands

  • insomnia bind
    To bind the AI, use: @insomnia bind channelid.
  • insomnia prefix
    Change the command prefix the bot uses in your server.
  • insomnia setup
    Begin the welcome setup for your server.

    Moderation Commands

  • insomnia ban
    Bans a mentioned user. Currently does not require reason (no mod-log).
  • insomnia case
    Check a case.
  • insomnia check
    Checks the guild for any user accounts younger than the minimum account age.
  • insomnia history
    Check the history for the mentioned member.
  • insomnia kick
    Kicks a mentioned user. Currently does not require reason (no mod-log).
  • insomnia lockdown
    Lock/unlock the selected channel.
  • insomnia prune
    Prunes a certain amount of messages w/o filter.
  • insomnia reason
    Edit the reason field from a case.
  • insomnia softban
    Softbans a mentioned user. Currently does not require reason (no mod-log).
  • insomnia unban
    Unbans the mentioned user.
  • insomnia warn
    Warns the mentioned member.
  • insomnia toggleinvites
    Enable/Disable Auto Deletion of Invite Links. Insomnia Mods and above can bypass this.
